
The first page of this application is the index.html page. Once the user clicks on "Rent" they are taken to the next page
where they can search on the last name. The matching data will be loaded from a json file and the user can select a the
rent form button. Once they click on rent form their information such as name, address, etc is pre-populated and they get
to choose their rental vehicle type, additional options and the number of days to rent. They can click cancel to go back to
the search results (the fields are cleared between this process). Or once they fill in their order they can click submit.
